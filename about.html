History of Barangay 1-B

Barangay 1-B San Pablo City has a rich history that dates back to the early days of the city’s development. The barangay was one of the original settlements in the area, and it has played a significant role in the city’s growth and development. According to the 1990 census, the population of Barangay 1-B was 3,758, which grew to 4,002 by 2020, an increase of 244 people over the course of 30 years. The latest census figures in 2020 denote a negative growth rate of 1.31%, or a decrease of 258 people, from the previous population of 4,260 in 2015.

The barangay is also home to various businesses, including small-scale industries, retail stores, and restaurants. The residents of Barangay 1-B are a mix of long-time residents and newcomers, with a diverse range of occupations and backgrounds.

In terms of education, the barangay has several schools, including public and private institutions, offering a range of educational programs and services. The city’s healthcare system is also well-established, with several hospitals and medical centers providing medical services to the residents.

Overall, Barangay 1-B San Pablo City has a rich history and a strong sense of community, with a mix of old and new developments that have shaped the area into what it is today.
